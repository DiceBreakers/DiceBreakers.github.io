Dice Breakers:

A conversation starter game for people who enjoy introspection and getting to know others on a deeper level.

Select a category that works for your group and roll the dice to break the ice!


Ways to Play:

Nothing but time: Everyone answers.
Rapid Fire: Prompts only apply to dice roller.
Targeted: Rollers choice.
Solo: Join our community forums for discussion, or roll the dice and journal your response!

Categories:

  • New Friends - Prompts to get you talking!
  • Old Friends - Willing to be vulnerable?
  • Kids/Family - Appropriate for all ages!
  • Would You Rather? - This or that, and why?
  • Happy Thoughts - Positive reflections on life.
  • Date Night - Know each other a little more.
  • Hard Hitting - Reflect, and possibly cry.
