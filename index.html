Dice Breakers:

A conversation starter game for people who enjoy introspection and getting to know others on a deeper level.

Select a category that works for your group and roll the dice to break the ice!


Ways to Play:

Nothing but time: Everyone answers.
Rapid Fire: Prompts only apply to dice roller.
Targeted: Rollers choice.
Solo: Join our community forums for discussion, or roll the dice and journal your response!

Categories:

  • New Friends - Prompts to get you talking!
  • Old Friends - Willing to be vulnerable?
  • Kids/Family - Appropriate for all ages!
  • Would You Rather? - This or that, and why?
  • Happy Thoughts - Positive reflections on life.
  • Date Night - Know each other a little more.
  • Hard Hitting - Reflect, and possibly cry.
  • Politics - Want to think politically?
  • Silly - Why so serious?
  • Spirituality - What do you believe?
  • Musical - Inspire a playlist.
  • Random - Some of this, little of that.
  • Journaling - Sit and think.

    I'm told I need more words on my home page to describe what this site is for, but I really like how clean it looks, so here is some comically small, white writing. I hope this makes the search engine overlords happy! Dice Breakers started as game I made up that used a google sheet and some dice to pick activities when friends and I were indecisive. Although I didn't call it "Dice Breakers" until recently. A friend suggested creating a list of prompts for sharing/getting to know friends better to add to the list of activities. Anyway, I couldn't sleep one morning and started writing questions to add to the game, then the name D(ice) Breakers came to mind. I guess I liked the name enough to develop it from a Google Sheets game to a website? I'll probably see about turning it into an app, too, although I'm not exactly a skilled programmer. Do you think that's enough text to make search engines happy? I promise I'm a real website. My goal is to help inspire positive, thought provoking, or otherwise meaningful conversations.
